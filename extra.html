Some of my Favorite Things

Introduction

I have savored a variety of sushi types throughout my life. Sushi holds a special place in my heart, and I'd like to share some of my favorite sushi picks with you.




  1. Nigiri Sushi
    • A classic choice with a slice of raw fish over a small bed of rice.
    • Simple, yet delightful, allowing you to appreciate the freshness of the fish.
    • Wasabi and soy sauce are the perfect companions for this sushi.

  2. Maki Rolls
    • Colorful and flavorful rolls with various ingredients like fish, vegetables, and rice, wrapped in seaweed.
    • Comes in different types such as California rolls, spicy tuna rolls, and dragon rolls.
    • Perfect for those who enjoy a combination of textures and flavors in each bite.
